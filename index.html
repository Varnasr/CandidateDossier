What this is & why it matters

This tool turns a structured CSV into a print-ready A4 PDF dossier — one page per candidate — using a restrained professional palette, clean key–value grids, gold section badges, and page numbers. All phone numbers are mandatorily masked to the last four digits (e.g., ******2345) to protect privacy.

Required CSV headers (names are tolerant for spaces/underscores/case):
preference, name, category, caste, age, occupation, education, joined, phone, record, rationale, strengths, weaknesses, proposers, positions, elections
