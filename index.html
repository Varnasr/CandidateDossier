Ummidvaar Dastaavez — Candidate Dossiers

What this does and why it matters

This tool converts a structured CSV into a high-quality PDF dossier — one page (or more) per candidate — with consistent typography, clear sections, and page numbers. Each CSV row becomes a reusable content block.

Privacy-first: phone numbers are mandatorily masked to the last four digits (e.g., ******2345).

Required CSV headers (names are case/space/underscore tolerant):
preference, name, category, caste, age, occupation, education, joined, phone, record, rationale, strengths, weaknesses, proposers, positions, elections
